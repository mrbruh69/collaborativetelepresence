Impact on Media and Entertainment

With the use of collaborative telepresence, new technology allows for ground-breaking game and app designs to enter the market. Game developers constantly introduce new technology into their games for their playerbase to have a unique and fresh gaming experience. Media coverage will also become easier due to the increase in connectivity between individuals and groups.
