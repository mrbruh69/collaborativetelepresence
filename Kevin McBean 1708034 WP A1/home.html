Introduction

Collaborative Telepresence is what will allow people in different parts of the world to smoothly interact as if they were physically together, down to being able to feel one another's touch (C. Lathan & A. Maynard). It uses rapidly-developing technology like augmented and virtual reality in order to bridge the gap between physical and virtual interactions.
